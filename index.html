35 Years. Three Experts. One Mission.

Jim Lovelace founded Fredericksburg Golf Center in 1991. His son Steve started working there in 1992 and bought the business in 1997. What started as a local shop became one of the most decorated fitting operations in Virginia — earning PING's Power Fitter of the Year three consecutive years running.
